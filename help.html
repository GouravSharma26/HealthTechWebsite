Help & Support

Welcome to our Help & Support section. How can we help you?

How to Book an Appointment?
  • Go to the Doctor List Page.
  • Click on the desired Doctor's Tab.
  • Press Book Appointment and choose your date & time.
  • Click Confirm to finalize the appointment.
How to Find Nearby Doctors?
  • Navigate to the Home Page.
  • Allow Location Access if asked.
  • Click on Doctors Nearby to see nearby doctors.
How to Reset Password?
  • Go to the Login Page.
  • Click on Forgot Password?.
  • Enter your **Email Address and submit.
  • Follow the **Email Instructions to reset your password.
How to Update Doctor Profile?
  • Go to your Doctor Profile Page.
  • Click the Edit Button.
  • Make necessary changes.
  • Press **Save** to update your details.
How to Contact Support?
  • Go to the Contact Us Page.
  • Fill out the Support Form.
  • Submit your request and wait for a response.
How to Delete an Appointment?
  • Go to your Appointment History.
  • Select the appointment you want to delete.
  • Click the Delete Button.
  • Confirm the deletion.
How to Enable Dark Mode?
  • Go to the **Menu Tab.
  • Select Change Appearance.
  • Toggle to Dark Mode.
How to Cancel a Doctor Signup?
  • Click on the Profile Tab.
  • Select Delete Account.
  • Confirm your Cancellation Request.
How to Edit Contact Details?
  • Navigate to your Profile Page.
  • Click on Edit Profile.
  • Change your Phone Number or Email Address.
  • Press Save.
How to Logout from Account?
  • Click on the Menu Tab.
  • Press Logout Button.
  • You will be redirected to the Login Page.
